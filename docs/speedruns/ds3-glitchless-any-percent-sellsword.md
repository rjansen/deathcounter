## Build

### Leveling Strategy

- Primary stat: **Dexterity** to 38-40 (soft cap)
- Secondary stats (if souls allow): Vigor to ~14-20, Endurance to ~19-29
- First leveling (Firelink visit): DEX to ~30
- Second leveling (Firelink visit): DEX to ~39

### Why DEX?

Sharp Sellsword Twinblades have **A-scaling with Dexterity**. Unlike the Anri route (Luck),
DEX is a more general stat — it also improves cast speed and benefits other DEX weapons.
The build is simpler and more forgiving for learning.

### Weapon Properties

- Dual-wield curved swords (weapon art: L1 combo)
- L1 attacks hit **twice per input** — effectively doubles resin/buff value
- Highest sustained DPS of any weapon class when buffed
- Upgrades with **regular Titanite** (max +10, only +3 needed in speedrun route)
- Requires **Sharp Gem** + **Farron Coal** for Sharp infusion at Andre

---

## Weapon Acquisition

The Sellsword Twinblades are a **world pickup** in the Road of Sacrifices / Crucifixion Woods
area, near the Halfway Fortress bonfire. They are found on a corpse past the Black Knight area,
near where the Farron Coal is located.

**Important:** The runner does NOT start as Mercenary (which has Twinblades as starting weapon).
All routes use Assassin for Spook. The Twinblades are picked up mid-run.

## Route Overview

Phases 1-3 and 7-10 are nearly identical to the Anri route. Key differences are in Phases 4-6
where weapon acquisition and build setup differ.

### Phase 1: Cemetery of Ash + Firelink Shrine

1. Pick up Ashen Estus Flask
2. Unequip head/chest armor for faster rolls
3. **Boss: Iudex Gundyr** (Estoc + Black Firebombs)
4. Firelink Shrine: purchase 2 Homeward Bones

### Phase 2: High Wall of Lothric (First Visit)

5. Pick up Gold Pine Resin
6. Descend via Spook to fountain area
7. Pick up 1 Ember
8. Kill Emma (Black Firebomb + charged R2s) to get Basin of Vows + Small Lothric Banner
9. **Boss: Dancer of the Boreal Valley** (Estoc)
10. Proceed up ladder into Lothric Castle

### Phase 3: Lothric Castle / Consumed King's Garden / Untended Graves

11. Navigate Lothric Castle toward Consumed King's Garden
12. **Boss: Oceiros, the Consumed King** (Estoc)
13. Access Untended Graves
14. **Boss: Champion Gundyr** (Estoc)
15. Homeward Bone / Darksign to return

### Phase 4: Vordt + Undead Settlement + Road of Sacrifices

16. Return to High Wall
17. **Boss: Vordt of the Boreal Valley** (Estoc + Gold Pine Resin)
18. Take banner to Undead Settlement
19. Pick up **Sharp Gem** (Crystal Lizard near Dilapidated Bridge)
20. Navigate to Road of Sacrifices
21. Pick up **Farron Coal** (behind Black Knight near Halfway Fortress)
22. Pick up **Sellsword Twinblades** (corpse near Farron Coal area)
23. Pick up Divine Braille Tome
24. Homeward Bone to Firelink

### Phase 5: Firelink Shopping + Build Setup

25. Sell boss souls and Embers for souls
26. Give **Farron Coal** to Blacksmith Andre
27. Infuse Sellsword Twinblades with **Sharp Gem** at Andre
28. Purchase Titanite Shards, upgrade to **Sharp Sellsword Twinblades +3**
29. Give Divine Braille Tome to Irina of Carim
30. Purchase Tears of Denial + Saint's Ring from Irina
31. Equip Saint's Ring, attune Tears of Denial
32. Level DEX to ~30

### Phase 6: Crystal Sage + Deacons

33. Warp to Halfway Fortress
34. **Boss: Crystal Sage** (Sharp Sellsword Twinblades +3)
35. Proceed to Cathedral of the Deep
36. **Boss: Deacons of the Deep** (obtain Small Doll)
37. Homeward Bone to Firelink

### Phase 7: Farron Keep + Catacombs + Irithyll

38. Navigate Catacombs of Carthus
39. **Boss: Abyss Watchers** (Lord of Cinder)
40. **Boss: High Lord Wolnir**
41. Enter Irithyll of the Boreal Valley
42. **Boss: Pontiff Sulyvahn**

### Phase 8: Profaned Capital + Anor Londo

43. Navigate to Profaned Capital
44. **Boss: Yhorm the Giant** (Storm Ruler)
45. Navigate to Anor Londo
46. **Boss: Aldrich, Devourer of Gods**

### Phase 9: Grand Archives + Endgame

47. Return to Lothric Castle
48. **Boss: Dragonslayer Armour**
49. Grand Archives
50. **Boss: Twin Princes (Lothric, Younger Prince)**
51. Place all Lords of Cinder on their thrones

### Phase 10: Kiln of the First Flame

52. Warp to Kiln of the First Flame
53. **Boss: Soul of Cinder**
54. Link the First Flame
55. Timer ends when IGT is confirmed on Load Game screen

## Route Advantages

- **Highest sustained DPS** — L1 dual-hit combos with buffs are devastating
- **Beginner-friendly** — high DPS compensates for suboptimal play
- **No NPC questline dependency** — weapon is a guaranteed world pickup
- **DEX is general-purpose** — useful stat beyond just this weapon
- **Forgiving upgrade path** — regular Titanite Shards are cheap and plentiful
- **Double buff value** — resins apply to both blades, effectively doubled on L1

## Route Disadvantages

- **~2 minutes slower** than Anri route at competitive level (43:21 vs 41:25)
- **Extra pickups required** — Sharp Gem, Farron Coal, Twinblades (3 pickups vs 1 NPC kill)
- **No passive healing** — no Blessed effect like Anri's Sword
- **Shorter range** — curved swords have less reach than straight swords
- **Uses Estoc for 5 bosses** — first 5 bosses fought with weaker starting weapon
- **Andre interaction** — must give coal + infuse + upgrade (more shop time than Anri route)
